The Team

See who makes up the greatest FRC team there is!

Build Team

The Build team is the group that builds the robot once the design has been planned. This group is crucial to the various competitions after the 6-week build period.

The members of the group use different tools on both metal and non-metal parts and learn about different mechanisms for maximum efficiency. Electronic work in regards to connecting all of the components together is also a significant part of the group.

    Coding Team

    The Coding/Programming team is the group that writes code for the robot to interpret and let us move it around using a controller, perform actions during autonomous mode, etc.

    Arguably, we have a skilled team of coders. This means two things. One, our team usually understands how a goal should be reached programmatically with different solutions. Two, we are able to train other members who wish to program, but don't know how.

    Media Team

    The Media team records all the all of the information from events and trainings we attend to the website. They capture picture and video of events and put them onto the our YouTube channel. Bascally, the Media team's goal is to make their team look as great as possible.

    Business Team

    The Business team is responsible for fundraising, awards, public relations, and community out reach. This year all the members of business team are new to FRC and are learning the ropes under the tutelage of coach Lynn. The objective for the business team this year is to raise $4,000 to go towards competition entry fees.
