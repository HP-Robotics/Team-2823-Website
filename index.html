Welcome to Highland Park Robotics

Highland Park Team 2823 - The Automatons - is a team of Highland students that compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition league each year. The team meets each Thursday after school in the Wood Shop throughout the year. During 'build season' (six weeks starting in January), the team meets Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday, while we build our robot.

Join Us!

The team is open to all students. To join, simply come to the wood shop after school on any Thursday. You can also sign up electronically by clicking the button below!

We have good coders. Seriously.

Arguably, we have a skilled team of coders. This means two things. One, our team usually understands how a goal should be reached programmatically with different solutions. Two, we are able to train other members who wish to program, but don't know how.

Our build team is also good. See for yourself.

The members of the group use different tools on both metal and non-metal parts and learn about different mechanisms for maximum efficiency. Electronic work in regards to connecting all of the components together is also a significant part of the group.
